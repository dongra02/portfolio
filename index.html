About Me

I am a nonprofit and education operations professional turned junior software developer. Having witnessed the challenges and opportunities a strong tech foundation can present for an organization; I was driven to pursue software development.

An international relocation to Beirut, Lebanon provided me an opportunity to dive full-time into learning the basics, and ultimately to completing the Software Engineering Immersive course offered by General Assembly. In addition to starting a foundation of skills to build on, the program offered me opportunities to collaborate using a variety of industry tools.

Today, my goal is to join an organization where I can contribute while greedily learning from those building and maintaining the technology that connects the programming to the community.
